oh, and by the way, this shit hits you HARD. i was pretty high on my first listen, though i was crying halfway through the song. i've been in a lot of pretty fucked up positions, so this album kind of just spoke to me in a way that no other album really did. it really makes you think about yourself... and that's just what makes it amazing.

the only problem is that the artwork is softcore... actually, i won't finish that.
