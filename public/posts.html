Tan (@tantoaura)

I'm just so surprised that out of all ways to end my year, the thing that's going to end my year is an obsession with a song created by somebody in the MLP fandom. An album that literally has near zero ties to MLP, apart from being a maybe loosely based fanfiction? I guess?

Maybe it's just that it hits home for me. I'm not sure. But "Revenge Fantasy" is probably one of the most attaching songs I've ever heard. It's a great duality between subtle and out there. The intro is something I can't stop listening to...

The album's story elements didn't really hit me as hard as it did before (maybe because I didn't read the novella along with it), though this album has quickly made an impact on me. Maybe I just feel parelells to some of the song's themes, as somebody who's intrusively had a messy, negative mindset towards myself and how I've kind of connected the album's theme of Trauma to something I can get attached to?

I'm not sure. It won't easy be to just say "this album is really good, pick it up!" because it has like a thousand content warnings and some of the songs are pretty far from easy to stomach... But regardless. It's a song I hold close to me right now. Maybe I'm just addicted to what the songs on this album make me feel.

If you're curious what album I'm talking about, it's under the status page...

god, no the FUCK this is not like wss.

(18+) read more

i've read about the album top to bottom. skimmed a bit through the novella that came with the album out of curiosity, looked at some of the visuals created for all the songs, read about the album history, etc... most of the album's direction came after the creator (a brony btw) had a dream about a god turning into somebody they hurt in their past, i think. that kind of makes it hit harder tbh, but i have my own struggles, so the album reached me in my own way.

oh, and by the way, this shit hits you HARD. i was pretty high on my first listen, though i was crying halfway through the song. i've been in a lot of pretty fucked up positions, so this album kind of just spoke to me in a way that no other album really did. it really makes you think about yourself... and that's just what makes it amazing.

the only problem is that the artwork is softcore... actually, i won't finish that.
